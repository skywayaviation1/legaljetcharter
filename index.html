About Legal Jet Charter

Legal Jet Charter is a verification tool for the charter industry — for brokers conducting due diligence, for travelers booking flights, and for anyone who needs to confirm that an aircraft is authorized to fly under 14 CFR Part 121 or Part 135. Inclusion confirms the operator–aircraft combination appears on the FAA's most recent published list.

The Watchlist is editorially maintained and independent of the FAA registry. It surfaces operators with known financial, safety, or regulatory concerns. Inclusion on the Watchlist does not contradict the FAA listing — the two layers coexist.
